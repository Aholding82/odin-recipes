Chankonabe

Description

Chankonabe when translated to japanese means Sumo (chanko) hot pot (nabe) and is a hot pot dish eaten by sumos. It involves chicken meat balls for protein and lots of vegetables , all of which are cooked within a dashi based soup an served typically with a side of rice.

Ingredients

  • Dashi Stock Powder
  • Sake
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Ginger
  • Garlic

  • Chicken Mince meat
  • Spring Onion
  • Cornflour
  • Egg
  • Panko Breadcrumbs

  • Tofu (medium firmness)
  • Wom Bok Cabbage
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Daikon Radish
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Bok Choy

Steps

  1. Make the soup base by combining the Dashi Stock, Soy Sauce, Mirin, Sake, Garlic and Ginger.
  2. Let the soup base cook together whilst preparing your other ingredients
  3. Peel the carrots and the Daikon then slice them into bite size pieces and dice your onion.
  4. Combine all meatball ingredients into a bowl until all ingredients are mixed together
  5. Slice up your Cabbage into edible pieces, remove the end of the bokchoy and wash the leaves, halve your shittake mushrooms and slice your tofu into 1-inch pieces
  6. After all of your ingredients are prepared begin cooking
  7. Start with the Onion, Carrot and Daikon as they have the longest cooking time. cook for 5 minutes
  8. Add the meatballs using either the hand-squeeze, rolling, or the teaspoon method
  9. After adding the meatballs add the cabbage along with the mushrooms
  10. Cook for another 3 minutes then add your tofu and the Bok Choy
  11. Cook for another 2 minutes then serve with rice
