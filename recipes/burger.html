Burger Recipe

Simple recipe for cooking a delicious burger

Ingredients

  • Buns (Brioche or Potato)
  • Full Fat Beef Mince (around 100-150g of mince is needed for every patty depending on the bun size
  • Cheese Slices (American Cheese is preferrable)
  • Brown Onion
  • Dill Pickles
  • Tomatoes
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Form around 150g of the beef mince into a circular patty, the size should be slightly larger than the buns that are being used (they shrink with cooking)
  2. Prepare your burger toppings, slice the Tomatoes and the pickles then dice your Lettuce and Onions
  3. Melt two teaspoons of butter in a frying pan over medium heat then toast the buns in the melted butter
  4. Heat the pan to high then season both sides of your patties with salt and pepper and add them to the pan
  5. While the meat is cooking assemble the burger. Add Ketchup to the bottom bun, making sure to uniformly cover the entire bun then add the Onions, Pickles and finally Lettuce
  6. For the top bun cover uniformly spread the mayonnaise then add the tomato slices
  7. Flip the burger patties when the first side is browned then add the cheese on top to melt while the other side cooks
  8. The patty should be browned after two minutes, though you cna use the level of melted the cheese is as an indicator
  9. When the patty is cooked at it to the bottom bun on top of the lettuce, let it rest for a minute then add the top bun and serve
